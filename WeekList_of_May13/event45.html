Teaching Ideas Workshop-Teaching Controversial Issues in the Classroom
Start: 12:00
End: 14:00
Location: Perkins Library 217
Description: Controversy, conflict, and disagreement are integral elements of college teaching. Instructors can use controversy deliberately, through a system of intentional engagement, to promote critical thinking in students. In this workshop, participants will consider ways to anticipate controversy and use it productively, through a repertoire of approaches that avoid antagonism and promote engagement.
