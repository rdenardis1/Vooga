Description: The Career Center welcomes Fannie Mitchell Expert in Residence Jeff Weiss, T'94 and Rebecca Rosen to discuss careers related to Government, International Affairs and the Environment. REGISTER: Students can sign-up for individual appointments or for the group session through Duke eRecruiting. Search Jeff Weiss or Rebecca Rosen. Jeff Weiss is the Senior Director for Technical Barriers to Trade (TBT) at the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR). Rebecca Rosen is a Republican Professional Staff Member and Advisor to Senator Lisa Murkowski, on the Senate Energy & Natural Resources Committee, where she has primary responsibility over a variety of issues in the oil & gas world. LOCATION: Career Center, Smith Warehouse Bay 5 2nd floor.
