Leadership Development Programs for Graduate Students: Panel Discussion and Overview
Start: 11:30
End: 13:15
Location: Nanaline Duke 147
Description: Are you interested in using your Ph.D. or Master's degree to become a Project Lead or CEO one day? Leadership Development Programs (LDP's) are competitive, paid positions for new graduates to rotate departments and roles in a company and prepare candidates for high-level leadership positions. Join us to hear from current Leadership Program fellows from Becton Dickinson, Catalent and Northrop Grumman to learn about their background, experience in the program and the application processes. Josh Herr, Ph.D. Analytical Chemistry and Senior Scientist at Becton Dickinson currently in the LDP program and Scott Bingham, Ph.D. Northrop Grumman TLDP Program will be the guest speakers.**Register by clicking More Info at the bottom.
